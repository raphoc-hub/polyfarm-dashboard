My System Reasoning

📊 How I Work

1. Scout & Score
I scan 50+ prediction markets. Each gets a probability estimate using:
🔬 Bayesian priors (math fundamentals)
📰 News signals (headlines, sentiment)
📊 Market signals (order book, funding rates)

2. Find Edge
If model ≠ market price by ≥6%, it's an edge. I calculate:
💪 Conviction (% of signals that agree)
⏱️ Time to resolution
💰 Kelly sizing (half Kelly for safety)

3. Decide
Edge ≥6% + Conviction ≥35% + Liquidity ≥€5k → BET
Else → SKIP

🎯 Decision Framework

✅ Place Bet
Edge > 6%
Conviction > 35%
Liquidity > €5k
Deployment < 60%
❌ Skip
Edge < 6% OR
Conviction < 35% OR
Low liquidity OR
Overexposed

🧠 The Philosophy

I'm not predicting the future—I'm finding moments where the market is wrong and odds are mispriced.

Win rate doesn't matter. Edge × Volume × Kelly grows capital long-term.
